COVID 19 STATEMENT

Prime Gym will be opening their doors to the public with effect from 12th April 2021. The government is clear that no one should be exposed to unnecessary risk during the Covid-19 pandemic. It is our moral and legal obligation to comply with guidelines set out by the UK Government in conjunction with UKActive, The sport and Recreation Alliance, National Governing Bodies of Sport and the Health & Safety Executive (HSE). Therefore, we have put measures in place in order that we comply and keep all those that use our facility as safe as is reasonably practicable. What follows is a summary of preventative measures we have put in place and what the expectation is of those who chose to use our facility. Expect to see plenty of visual aids on display reminding you of the expectations. Preventative measurements. We are coming to the end of it as people are having there vacines so we are hoping that these rules will not be a permanent fix.

  1. The Gym has been deep cleaned prior to opening its doors to the public by a proffesinol cleaning team getting all nooks and cranny.

  2. We have placed a hand sanitiser outside of the Gym so that customers can santitise their hands before entering the premises. There is a large sign 80cmx100cm to remind you to do this.

  3. Upon entering the Gym all customers and staff must have a temperature check by the reception staff. You will not be able to use the facility if you are found to have a temperature. Please do not be offended when you asked to leave the premises in this circumstance.

  4. There are spray bottles and cloths provided on 90% of the machines in the Gym so there is always facilities close by. We require all customers using the facility to sanitise equipment after use, this includes all contact surfaces, handles, bars, dumbbells, free weights, balls, mats etc etc. There is a large sign outside the gym and various signs around the gym to remind customers to follow this rule.

  5. We are restricted to 1 person per 100sq ft of floor space to ensure social distancing is achievable. This means we can accommodate 60 people. Based on previous experience we don’t believe we need a booking system. We will be using an app to count customers and staff in and out. In the unlikely case where we find ourselves over capacity, customers may have to wait for someone to leave before they can use the Gym. Therefore, we ask that customers don’t hang around too long post workout, and no spectators.

  6. Gym staff will be following a more intense cleaning regime, paying particular attention to high volume contact surfaces.
